Burger Recipe

Cook Time:

  • Prep: 10 min
  • Cook: 25 min
  • Total: 35 min

Ingredients:

  1. 1 package of ground beef
  2. 2 brioche buns
  3. 1 slice American Cheese
  4. 1 teaspoon salt

Directions:

  1. Form patty out of ground beef
  2. Heat grill to 200 degrees
  3. Cook beef patty until medium-well
  4. Toast brioche buns
  5. Place beef patty between buns
